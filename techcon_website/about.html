History


Founding:2010

n 2010, a group of visionary technologists and entrepreneurs came together with a common goal: to create a space where innovators could exchange ideas and showcase cutting-edge technologies. This led to the launch of TechCon, held in a modest venue with just 500 attendees. The first conference was centered around emerging fields like cloud computing, mobile applications, and early developments in AI.


Expansion:2012

By 2012, TechCon had already outgrown its original venue, attracting over 2,000 participants. The conference expanded its focus to include new topics like cybersecurity, big data analytics, and internet-of-things (IoT). That year, TechCon featured its first international keynote speaker, marking its growing global appeal.


Global Reach:2018

In 2018, TechCon reached new heights, hosting over 50,000 attendees from around the world. For the first time, the event featured global satellite conferences in Tokyo, Berlin, and São Paulo, connecting innovators across continents. The key themes of the conference included AI, blockchain, and the growing impact of 5G networks.


Innovation & Sustainability: 2023

The latest edition of TechCon in 2023 embraced the theme of sustainability in tech. The conference featured discussions on the environmental impact of technology, and showcased innovations aimed at reducing the carbon footprint of the tech industry. With AI, green energy solutions, and quantum computing taking center stage, TechCon continued to be a driving force in shaping the future of technology.
